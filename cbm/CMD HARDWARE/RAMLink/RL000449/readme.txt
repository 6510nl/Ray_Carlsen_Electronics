This RAMLink appears to be an early model with a low serial number and a board 
date of 1990 REV D. It has an early DOS ROM V1.40 and uses the dual-voltage 
1581 type PS. 

Its early version SIMM board (date 1990) has the jumper intact to use 1M SIMMs, 
and no RTC.
