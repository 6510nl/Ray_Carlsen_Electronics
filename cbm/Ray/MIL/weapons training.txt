                                            6-21-04

This list of names were men stationed at Bravo Battery when I was 
there and that took weapons training at that time. The lists are
not complete, but simply the partial extract of orders I received 
at the time. I make this list available to those who may be 
interested in remembering those days and the people they met, and 
perhaps wanting to find "lost buddies".
